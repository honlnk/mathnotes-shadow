本文档整合了三角函数的 **加减乘除运算公式** 和 **恒等式**，涵盖了从基本恒等式到复杂公式的所有内容，帮助你更好地理解和应用三角函数。

---

## **1. 基本恒等式**

### **平方和公式**
- $$
  \sin^2{\theta} + \cos^2{\theta} = 1
  $$
- $$
  1 + \tan^2{\theta} = \sec^2{\theta}
  $$
- $$
  1 + \cot^2{\theta} = \csc^2{\theta}
  $$

### **倒数关系**
- $$
  \sin{\theta} = \frac{1}{\csc{\theta}}, \quad \cos{\theta} = \frac{1}{\sec{\theta}}, \quad \tan{\theta} = \frac{1}{\cot{\theta}}
  $$

### **商数关系**
- $$
  \tan{\theta} = \frac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}}, \quad \cot{\theta} = \frac{\cos{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}}
  $$

---

## **2. 和差公式**
- $$
  \sin{(A \pm B)} = \sin{A}\cos{B} \pm \cos{A}\sin{B}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{(A \pm B)} = \cos{A}\cos{B} \mp \sin{A}\sin{B}
  $$
- $$
  \tan{(A \pm B)} = \frac{\tan{A} \pm \tan{B}}{1 \mp \tan{A}\tan{B}}
  $$  
---

## **3. 倍角公式**

### **二倍角公式**
- $$
  \sin{2\theta} = 2\sin{\theta}\cos{\theta}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{2\theta} = \cos^2{\theta} - \sin^2{\theta} = 2\cos^2{\theta} - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2{\theta}
  $$
- $$
  \tan{2\theta} = \frac{2\tan{\theta}}{1 - \tan^2{\theta}}
  $$

### **三倍角公式**
- $$
  \sin{3\theta} = 3\sin{\theta} - 4\sin^3{\theta}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{3\theta} = 4\cos^3{\theta} - 3\cos{\theta}
  $$
- $$
  \tan{3\theta} = \frac{3\tan{\theta} - \tan^3{\theta}}{1 - 3\tan^2{\theta}}
  $$

---

## **4. 半角公式**

### **半角公式**
- $$
  \sin{\frac{\theta}{2}} = \pm \sqrt{\frac{1 - \cos{\theta}}{2}}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{\frac{\theta}{2}} = \pm \sqrt{\frac{1 + \cos{\theta}}{2}}
  $$
- $$
  \tan{\frac{\theta}{2}} = \pm \sqrt{\frac{1 - \cos{\theta}}{1 + \cos{\theta}}} = \frac{1 - \cos{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}} = \frac{\sin{\theta}}{1 + \cos{\theta}}
  $$

---

## **5. 积化和差公式**

### **积化和差**
- $$
  \sin{A}\sin{B} = \frac{1}{2} [\cos{(A - B)} - \cos{(A + B)}]
  $$
- $$
  \cos{A}\cos{B} = \frac{1}{2} [\cos{(A - B)} + \cos{(A + B)}]
  $$
- $$
  \sin{A}\cos{B} = \frac{1}{2} [\sin{(A + B)} + \sin{(A - B)}]
  $$

---

## **6. 和差化积公式**

### **和差化积**
- $$
  \sin{A} + \sin{B} = 2 \sin{\left( \frac{A + B}{2} \right)} \cos{\left( \frac{A - B}{2} \right)}
  $$
- $$
  \sin{A} - \sin{B} = 2 \cos{\left( \frac{A + B}{2} \right)} \sin{\left( \frac{A - B}{2} \right)}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{A} + \cos{B} = 2 \cos{\left( \frac{A + B}{2} \right)} \cos{\left( \frac{A - B}{2} \right)}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{A} - \cos{B} = -2 \sin{\left( \frac{A + B}{2} \right)} \sin{\left( \frac{A - B}{2} \right)}
  $$

---

## **7. 万能公式**

### **万能公式**（用 $\displaystyle \tan{\frac{\theta}{2}}$ 表示）
- $$
  \sin{\theta} = \frac{2\tan{\frac{\theta}{2}}}{1 + \tan^2{\frac{\theta}{2}}}
  $$
- $$
  \cos{\theta} = \frac{1 - \tan^2{\frac{\theta}{2}}}{1 + \tan^2{\frac{\theta}{2}}}
  $$
- $$
  \tan{\theta} = \frac{2\tan{\frac{\theta}{2}}}{1 - \tan^2{\frac{\theta}{2}}}
  $$

---

## **8. 特殊角的三角函数值**

### **常见角度值**
- $$
  \sin{0} = 0, \quad \sin{\frac{\pi}{6}} = \frac{1}{2}, \quad \sin{\frac{\pi}{4}} = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}, \quad \sin{\frac{\pi}{3}} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \quad \sin{\frac{\pi}{2}} = 1
  $$
- $$
  \cos{0} = 1, \quad \cos{\frac{\pi}{6}} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \quad \cos{\frac{\pi}{4}} = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}, \quad \cos{\frac{\pi}{3}} = \frac{1}{2}, \quad \cos{\frac{\pi}{2}} = 0
  $$

---

## **9. 三角函数的对称性**

### **奇偶性**
- $$
  \sin{(-\theta)} = -\sin{\theta}, \quad \cos{(-\theta)} = \cos{\theta}, \quad \tan{(-\theta)} = -\tan{\theta}
  $$

### **周期性**
- $$
  \sin{(\theta + 2\pi)} = \sin{\theta}, \quad \cos{(\theta + 2\pi)} = \cos{\theta}, \quad \tan{(\theta + \pi)} = \tan{\theta}
  $$

---

## **10. 三角函数的幂运算**

### **降幂公式**
- $$
  \sin^2{\theta} = \frac{1 - \cos{2\theta}}{2}
  $$
- $$
  \cos^2{\theta} = \frac{1 + \cos{2\theta}}{2}
  $$

---

## **11. 辅助角公式**

### **辅助角公式**
- $$
  a \sin x + b \cos x = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \sin(x + \phi), \quad \text{其中} \quad \phi = \arctan\left(\frac{b}{a}\right)
  $$
